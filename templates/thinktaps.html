ThinkTaps

Description

The mobile note-taking app was developed to provide users with a simple, intuitive platform for managing notes. Users can easily create, edit, and delete notes, with the ability to search and view past entries. The app restricts users from adding entries on past or future dates to maintain the reliability of data. Additionally, users can view entries made within the current day on the homepage.

Technologies Used

Flutter GitHub Firebase
